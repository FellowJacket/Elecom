About Us

What do we stand for?

In simple terms, we stand for you. Our philosophy at Elecoms is the conservation of all things to do with humanity. This company is essentially a living time capsule.

Centuries ago, people told their history through only spoken means. This proved to be a very terrible form of preserving history, and a plethora of important information was lost among the ages. So we developed books, a much more efficient method of documentation. Books evolved into television, among other recorded media. Information is stored throughout the internet, all over the world, and we here at Elecoms collect as much of it as we can and store it. In this sense, Elecoms is the natural next evolutionary step of books.

What does this mean for us?

Elecoms stores all sorts of information, including historical events, culturally significant media, and threats to people's lives. As we collect more and more information, government funded intelligence agencies are free to access our files, and develop their defenses accordingly. This ensures that you, the average citizen, is well protected against any possible future threats.
